About Technopreneur

Technopreneur, whose first edition in 2006 went on to win the best Yi-Net event at the CII National Summit in Mumbai, is a platform where technology meets entrepreneurship. The symposium scope is to provide a thematic umbrella for entrepreneurs to share their ground breaking ideas and their true life stories of triumph. Technopreneur 2016 will embrace this humble tradition and in addition intends to furnish training and skillful guidance to hungry minds seeking to captain an enterprise of their own, over a two day period filled with innovation and enlightenment.

Model Engineering College

In the 26 years since its inception, Govt. Model Engineering College (MEC), Kochi has carved a niche for itself as one of the best Engineering colleges in the state. Ranked 41 in India among the top engineering colleges by Outlook Magazine in 2015, MEC is also one of the most sought out colleges in India with the likes of Yahoo, Nvidia, Deloitte, Bosch, Broadcom, TCS, etc visiting the campus consistently. Along with its record breaking placements and academic excellence, the array of events it hosts has been praised for their caliber and dynamism, embracing the right mix of technology and innovation for a growing and constantly evolving community.
